📖 Project Overview

LegalEase is a modern online lawyer hiring platform that connects clients, businesses, and legal seekers with professional lawyers. The platform enables users to discover, hire, and interact with legal experts while providing lawyers with a marketplace to showcase their legal services.

The system includes role-based authentication, lawyer verification, hiring management, payment integration, analytics, comments, and secure dashboard functionality for Users, Lawyers, and Administrators.

🎯 Project Purpose

Traditional legal consultation is often limited to physical offices and law firms. LegalEase bridges this gap by providing:

Easy access to legal professionals
Secure lawyer hiring process
Online payment system
Lawyer profile management
Role-based dashboards
Transparent hiring history
Modern legal marketplace experience
